Our History

Two decades of creating smiles for millions of people with an inspiring experience and breakthrough business transformation, adding more to lives through sustained growth, celebrating the great taste by refreshing minds and playing to inspire like none other.

2000

Transcom Beverages Ltd (TBL) starts its journey as the Exclusive PepsiCo. Franchisee for Bangladesh region.

The journey begins with two plants, one located in The Tejgaon Industrial area with 400 BPM (bottle per minute) and one in Chittagong with 400 BPM (bottle per minute). Initially these plants started off with GRB (Glass return bottle) and in the following year pet line was added in the production line.

2004

In 2004, The Tejgaon plant was shifted to Konabari in its own acquired land and rebranded itself as Dhaka plant. This new plant was equipped with 630 BPM line and shrink film packaging.

2005

TBL has achieved subsequently the Pepsico prestigious award “Bottler of the year” 9 times in the year of 2005, 2008-2014, and 2016. Huskey line were installed in Dhaka plant.

2009

A transition from 400 BPM to 1430 BPM to increase production capacity. Received International Bottler of the year in 2009.

2013

Aquafina production line installed in Dhaka plant.

2014

TBL acquired a plant from Global beverages and started Bagher Bazar plant operation in order to supply the growing demand for the beverages industry.

CAN line and PET line of Slice installed at Bagher Bazar plant.

400 BPM PET line were installed at Chittagong plant.

2015

7up achieved best beverage brand 10 times in a row by the brand forum.

2016-2021

Received International Bottler of the year in 2016. Total capacity enhanced to 6660 in three plants.

Additional 13 bigha land acquired to extend with Dhaka plant.

Achieved AIB Gold award in 2021.

Best Employer Brand 2015 in Asia Region.
